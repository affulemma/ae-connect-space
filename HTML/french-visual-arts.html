A Short Story

A young designer wanted to work with international clients and showcase creative work across Africa and beyond. One skill opened unexpected doors. That skill was language. French is spoken by millions of people around the world and can create opportunities in education, business, diplomacy, tourism, and international careers.


What Is French?

French is an international language spoken across Africa, Europe, North America, and many other parts of the world. Studying French helps students communicate with a wider audience and access global opportunities.

Something Most Students Don't Know

French is one of the most valuable languages for students in West Africa. Many neighbouring countries are French-speaking, making the language useful for trade, networking, travel, business, education, and international opportunities.
