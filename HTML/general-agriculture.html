GENERAL AGRICULTURE

A Short Story

A student once thought agriculture was only about farming. Later, the student discovered that agriculture powers food production, business, technology, exports, manufacturing, and national development. Today, some of the world's biggest businesses are connected to agriculture. Agriculture is much bigger than most people imagine.


What Is General Agriculture?

General Agriculture is the study of crop production, soil management, farm operations, agricultural economics, environmental sustainability, and modern farming practices. It helps students understand how food and raw materials are produced for society.

Something Most Students Don't Know

Agriculture is one of the few industries that combines science, technology, engineering, business, entrepreneurship, and environmental management. As populations grow, the demand for food, innovation, and sustainable farming continues to increase.
