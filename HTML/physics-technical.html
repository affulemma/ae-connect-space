PHYSICS

A Short Story

A student wondered how airplanes fly, why bridges stay standing, and how electricity powers entire cities. The answers were hidden in Physics. Physics helps us understand the laws that govern matter, energy, motion, and the universe itself.


What Is Physics?

Physics is the study of matter, energy, forces, motion, electricity, waves, and the physical laws that explain how the world works. It is one of the foundations of engineering, technology, and modern science.

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think Physics is only about calculations. In reality, Physics explains the technology we use every day, from mobile phones and computers to satellites, vehicles, and renewable energy systems.
