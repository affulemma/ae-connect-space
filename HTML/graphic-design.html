GRAPHIC DESIGN

A Short Story

A student noticed that every successful brand had a logo, attractive posters, social media graphics, and visual identity. Behind all those visuals was Graphic Design. Graphic Design combines creativity and communication to help people share ideas, tell stories, and build brands.


What Is Graphic Design?

Graphic Design is the art of creating visual content to communicate messages. It combines creativity, technology, typography, colour, images, and design principles to solve communication problems.

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think Graphic Design is only about making posters. In reality, graphic designers help build brands, create user experiences, design websites, develop advertising campaigns, and influence how people interact with products and businesses.
