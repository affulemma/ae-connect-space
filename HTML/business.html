BUSINESS

Business is for students interested in entrepreneurship, finance, management, accounting, marketing, economics, banking, leadership, and wealth creation. Before choosing Business, remember that English Language, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science, and Social Studies are compulsory for all SHS students.
