TWI

A Short Story

A young graduate attended a community meeting to discuss a development project. Many of the people present could not speak English fluently, but they spoke Twi. Because the graduate could communicate effectively in Twi, he gained the trust of the community and successfully led the project. Sometimes opportunities come not because of what you know, but because of how well you can connect with people.


What Is Twi?

Twi is one of the most widely spoken Ghanaian languages. Studying Twi helps students understand language, culture, communication, traditions, and the history of Ghanaian societies. It also strengthens speaking, writing, reading, and communication skills.


What Will You Study In SHS?

As an SHS student, you may study topics such as:

  • Twi Grammar
  • Reading and Comprehension
  • Essay Writing
  • Oral Literature
  • Proverbs and Idioms
  • Traditional Stories
  • Ghanaian Culture
  • Translation Skills
  • Communication Skills
  • Language Development

Career Opportunities

  • Teacher

  • Journalist
  • Translator
  • Public Relations Officer
  • Communications Specialist
  • Researcher
  • Community Development Officer
  • Cultural Affairs Officer

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students underestimate local languages because they focus only on international languages. However, the ability to communicate effectively with local communities is a powerful skill in leadership, business, media, politics, development work, and public service. Understanding your language and culture can create opportunities that many people overlook.
