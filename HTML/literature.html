ENGLISH LITERATURE

A Short Story

A young student once read a novel about a person who overcame poverty, rejection, and failure to become successful. Years later, whenever life became difficult, the lessons from that story gave the student courage to keep moving forward. That is the power of Literature. Sometimes a story can teach lessons that facts alone cannot teach.


What Is Literature?

Literature is the study of written works such as novels, plays, poetry, and short stories. It helps students understand human experiences, cultures, emotions, communication, and creative expression through language.


What Will You Study In SHS?

As an SHS student, you may study topics such as:

  • Poetry
  • Drama
  • Prose Fiction
  • Literary Appreciation
  • Literary Devices
  • Character Analysis
  • Themes and Symbolism
  • African Literature
  • World Literature
  • Critical Writing

Career Opportunities

Literature can lead to careers such as:

  • Lawyer
  • Journalist
  • Author
  • Teacher
  • Lecturer
  • Editor
  • Public Relations Specialist
  • Communications Officer
  • Content Creator
  • Media Professional
  • Diplomat

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think Literature is only for people who love reading novels. In reality, Literature develops communication, analysis, persuasion, creativity, and critical thinking skills. These are some of the most valuable skills in law, media, leadership, education, business, and many other professions.
