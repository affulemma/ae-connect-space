ACCOUNTING

A Short Story

Two businesses started with the same amount of money. One owner carefully recorded income, expenses, profits, and losses. The other owner kept no records and spent money without tracking it. A few years later, one business was growing while the other was struggling. The difference was not luck. The difference was understanding Accounting.


What Is Accounting?

Accounting is the process of recording, classifying, summarizing, and interpreting financial information. It helps individuals, businesses, and organizations understand their financial position and make informed decisions.


What Will You Study In SHS?

As an SHS student, you may study topics such as:

  • Bookkeeping
  • Journals and Ledgers
  • Trial Balance
  • Cash Book
  • Bank Reconciliation
  • Financial Statements
  • Depreciation
  • Control Accounts
  • Partnership Accounts
  • Accounting Principles

Career Opportunities

Accounting can lead to careers such as:

  • Accountant
  • Auditor
  • Financial Analyst
  • Investment Analyst
  • Banker
  • Tax Consultant
  • Chief Financial Officer (CFO)
  • Business Consultant
  • Entrepreneur
  • Finance Manager

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think Accounting is only about calculations. In reality, Accounting is the language of business. Every successful organization relies on accurate financial information to make decisions, manage risks, and plan for growth. Understanding Accounting gives you an advantage whether you become a business owner, manager, investor, or financial professional.
