ELECTIVE MATHEMATICS

A Short Story

A young entrepreneur wanted to know whether a business idea would make a profit. To answer that question, they needed calculations, forecasting, statistics, and financial analysis. That is where Elective Mathematics became important. Behind successful businesses, investments, and financial decisions is the ability to work with numbers confidently.


What Is Elective Mathematics?

Elective Mathematics is an advanced mathematics subject that develops analytical thinking, problem-solving abilities, and quantitative skills. It is highly useful in business, finance, economics, banking, technology, and data analysis.


What Will You Study In SHS?

As an SHS student, you may study topics such as:

  • Algebra
  • Functions
  • Trigonometry
  • Calculus
  • Probability
  • Statistics
  • Matrices
  • Vectors
  • Coordinate Geometry
  • Mathematical Modelling

Career Opportunities

Elective Mathematics can lead to careers such as:

  • Actuary
  • Economist
  • Data Analyst
  • Data Scientist
  • Financial Analyst
  • Investment Analyst
  • Banker
  • Statistician
  • Business Consultant
  • Entrepreneur

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think Elective Mathematics is only useful for engineers and scientists. In reality, it is one of the strongest foundations for business, finance, banking, economics, actuarial science, statistics, and data analytics. Many of the world's highest-paying careers rely heavily on mathematical thinking.
