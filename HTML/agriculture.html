AGRICULTURE

Agriculture is for students interested in farming, food production, agribusiness, animal production, environmental sustainability, and agricultural technology. The programme combines science, business, and practical skills to help students understand how food, animals, and natural resources are managed. Select a subject below to learn more.

🌾 General Agriculture

Learn crop production, soil management, farm operations, and agricultural business.

🐄 Animal Husbandry

Study animal breeding, nutrition, health, and livestock management.

⚛️ Physics

Understand energy, forces, machinery, electricity, and agricultural technology.

🧪 Chemistry

Understand chemical processes important in agriculture, soil science, and food production.

📐 Elective Mathematics

Develop analytical and quantitative skills useful in agriculture and agribusiness.
