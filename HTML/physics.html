PHYSICS

A Short Story

A student once looked at an airplane flying across the sky and wondered how something so heavy could stay in the air. Later, the same student became curious about electricity, smartphones, satellites, and space travel. The answer to all these questions led back to Physics. Physics helps us understand the laws that govern the universe and the technology that powers modern life.


What Is Physics?

Physics is the study of matter, energy, motion, forces, and the fundamental laws of nature. It explains how objects move, how machines work, how electricity flows, and how the universe behaves.


What Will You Study In SHS?

As an SHS student, you may study topics such as:

  • Motion and Forces
  • Energy and Work
  • Electricity
  • Magnetism
  • Waves and Sound
  • Light and Optics
  • Heat and Temperature
  • Electronics
  • Mechanics
  • Practical Experiments

Career Opportunities

Physics can lead to careers such as:

  • Engineer
  • Physicist
  • Aerospace Engineer
  • Pilot
  • Data Scientist
  • Electronics Engineer
  • Software Developer
  • Telecommunications Specialist
  • Research Scientist
  • Lecturer

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think Physics is only about calculations and formulas. In reality, Physics is the foundation of engineering, aviation, robotics, artificial intelligence, renewable energy, telecommunications, and space exploration. Many of today's technological breakthroughs are built on principles first discovered through Physics.
