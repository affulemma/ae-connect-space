ELECTIVE ICT

A Short Story

A student loved creating posters and social media designs. Later, the student discovered that many modern designers also use computers, software, websites, and digital tools to bring their ideas to life. That is where ICT becomes powerful.


What Is Elective ICT?

Elective ICT focuses on computer technology, digital tools, software applications, information systems, and problem-solving using technology. It helps students develop skills needed in the digital world.

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many creative careers today depend heavily on technology. Graphic design, animation, video editing, digital marketing, website design, and content creation all require ICT skills. Students who combine creativity with technology often have more opportunities.
