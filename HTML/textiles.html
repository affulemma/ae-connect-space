TEXTILES

A Short Story

A student admired beautiful fabrics and clothing designs but never imagined the amount of creativity and skill involved in producing them. From traditional African prints to modern fashion collections, textiles play a major role in culture, business, and design. Textiles turns creativity into products people use every day.


What Is Textiles?

Textiles is the study of fabrics, fibres, textile production, fabric decoration, dyeing, printing, and textile design. It combines creativity, technology, and practical production skills.

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think Textiles is only about making cloth. In reality, the textile industry is a global business involving fashion, manufacturing, branding, technology, sustainability, and entrepreneurship. Some of the world's largest brands are built around textile products.
