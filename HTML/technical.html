TECHNICAL STUDIES

Technical Studies is for students interested in engineering, construction, manufacturing, technology, design, innovation, and practical problem-solving. The programme develops analytical, technical, and hands-on skills that prepare students for engineering and technology-related careers. Select a subject below to learn more.

🏗️ Building Construction

Learn construction principles, building materials, structures, and site practices.

⚛️ Physics

Understand forces, motion, energy, electricity, and engineering applications.

⚙️ Engineering Science

Study engineering principles, machines, technology, and technical problem-solving.

🪵 Woodwork

Develop practical skills in woodworking, furniture production, and design.

🧪 Chemistry

Explore matter, materials, chemical processes, and industrial applications.

📐 Elective Mathematics

Build mathematical and analytical skills needed in engineering and technology.
