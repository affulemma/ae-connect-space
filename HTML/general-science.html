GENERAL SCIENCE

Before choosing General Science, remember that English Language, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science, and Social Studies are compulsory subjects for all SHS students. General Science is designed for students interested in medicine, engineering, technology, research, environmental science, agriculture, and innovation. Select an elective subject below to learn more.

⚗️ Chemistry

Study matter, reactions, atoms, compounds, and the science behind everyday life.

⚡ Physics

Understand forces, motion, energy, electricity, and how the universe works.

🧬 Biology

Explore living organisms, human life, plants, animals, and health sciences.

🌍 Geography

Understand climate, environmental systems, resources, and sustainability.

💻 Elective ICT

Learn computing, digital technology, programming, and innovation.

📐 Elective Mathematics

Develop advanced mathematical skills for science, engineering, and technology.
