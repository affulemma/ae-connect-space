CHEMISTRY

A Short Story

A student once wondered why iron rusts, why medicines work, and why soap removes dirt. At first, these seemed like ordinary everyday events. But behind each of them was Chemistry. The more the student learned, the more they realized that Chemistry is happening around us every single day. From the food we eat to the air we breathe, Chemistry helps explain the world.


What Is Chemistry?

Chemistry is the study of matter, its composition, properties, structure, and the changes it undergoes. It helps us understand substances, reactions, materials, medicines, and many processes that affect daily life.


What Will You Study In SHS?

As an SHS student, you may study topics such as:

  • Atomic Structure
  • Chemical Bonding
  • States of Matter
  • Acids and Bases
  • Chemical Reactions
  • Organic Chemistry
  • Electrolysis
  • Periodic Table
  • Energy Changes
  • Laboratory Techniques

Career Opportunities

Chemistry can lead to careers such as:

  • Medical Doctor
  • Pharmacist
  • Chemical Engineer
  • Biochemist
  • Laboratory Scientist
  • Food Scientist
  • Environmental Scientist
  • Research Scientist
  • Forensic Scientist
  • Lecturer

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think Chemistry is only about formulas and laboratory work. In reality, Chemistry is one of the foundation subjects for medicine, pharmacy, engineering, agriculture, food science, environmental science, and many modern industries. Understanding Chemistry opens doors to some of the most impactful careers in the world.
