In cloud computing practice, industry projects, professional services, research, operations, and career development, this skill helps students connect academic knowledge with practical results.

Today, employers want graduates who can solve problems, use modern tools, communicate clearly, and show evidence of what they can do. AWS matters because it turns knowledge into practical value and helps students compete for internships, projects, and entry-level opportunities.
