GENERAL ARTS

Before choosing General Arts, remember that English Language, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science, and Social Studies are compulsory subjects for all SHS students. General Arts is one of the most flexible programmes in SHS and opens doors to careers in business, law, governance, economics, education, media, diplomacy, development, and many more. Select an elective subject below to learn more.

📈 Economics

Understand money, markets, businesses, and how economies work.

🌍 Geography

Study the earth, environment, climate, population, and resources.

🏛 Government

Learn how countries are governed, politics, constitutions, and leadership.

✝ CRS

Explore Christian teachings, morality, and religious studies.

📚 English Literature

Study novels, plays, poetry, and critical thinking.

🏺 History

🇫🇷 French
