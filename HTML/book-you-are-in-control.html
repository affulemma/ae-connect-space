A.E CONNECT BOOK

YOU ARE IN CONTROL: PICK YOUR OWN COURSE

A course-choice guide by Afful Emmanuel for students who want to understand their interests, strengths, goals, and future before choosing an academic path.

WHAT THIS BOOK IS ABOUT

This book helps JHS and SHS students understand that choosing a course is not something to follow blindly. It teaches students how to think about their interests, abilities, goals, course options, career prospects, and personal growth before deciding.

The message is simple: students are not powerless. With the right information and confidence, they can take control of their academic future.

WHO THIS BOOK IS FOR

  • JHS students preparing for SHS course selection.
  • SHS students thinking about university programs.
  • Parents and guardians guiding students.
  • Teachers, mentors, and career clubs supporting young people.

HOW THIS BOOK WILL HELP STUDENTS

01

Understand personal interests, strengths, and goals before choosing a course.

02

Know that SHS courses are stepping stones, not lifelong sentences.

03

Compare course options with career opportunities and future plans.

04

Build confidence to make informed decisions instead of following pressure.
