FINANCIAL ACCOUNTING

Financial Accounting helps students understand how businesses record transactions, prepare financial statements, and communicate financial performance clearly.

What It Covers

Learn journals, ledgers, trial balance, income statements, balance sheets, cash books, and basic accounting principles.

Why It Matters

Financial Accounting builds the foundation for business, accounting, finance, entrepreneurship, auditing, and management decisions.

Where It Can Lead

This subject can support pathways into accounting, banking, auditing, business administration, finance, and entrepreneurship.
