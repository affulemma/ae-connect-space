VISUAL ARTS

Visual Arts is for students who are creative, imaginative, and interested in design, fashion, drawing, communication, technology, and artistic expression. The programme combines creativity with practical skills and can lead to careers in art, media, architecture, design, technology, and entrepreneurship. Select a subject below to learn more.

🎨 Graphic Design

Learn visual communication, branding, digital design, and creative problem-solving.

🧵 Textiles

Explore fabric design, textile production, fashion, and creative industries.

🧺 Basketry

Learn traditional and modern weaving techniques and product creation.

👜 Leatherwork

Study leather design, craftsmanship, and product manufacturing.

🗿 Sculpture

Develop skills in three-dimensional artistic design and creative expression.

🏺 Ceramics

Learn pottery, ceramic production, and artistic design techniques.

📐 Elective Mathematics

Build analytical and quantitative skills useful in architecture, design, and technology.

💻 Elective ICT

Learn digital skills, design software, technology applications, and creative tools.

📚 English Literature

Develop communication, storytelling, critical thinking, and creative writing skills.

📈 Economics

Understand markets, entrepreneurship, business, and financial decision-making.

🇫🇷 French

Develop language skills that can create international opportunities and cultural understanding.
