A.E CONNECT BOOK

FROM HIGH SCHOOL TO FREEDOM: WHAT NO ONE TOLD YOU ABOUT TERTIARY LIFE

A tertiary-life guide by Afful Emmanuel for freshers and continuing students who want to handle campus freedom with wisdom, discipline, and purpose.

WHAT THIS BOOK IS ABOUT

This book prepares students for the transition from high school into tertiary life. It speaks honestly about freedom without supervision, personal responsibility, time management, learning how to learn, campus traps, friendships, GPA, first class goals, and life beyond a degree.

It helps students enter campus with awareness instead of confusion.

WHO THIS BOOK IS FOR

  • SHS graduates preparing for university, college, or technical school.
  • Freshers who want to avoid common campus mistakes.
  • Continuing students who want to reset their academic life.
  • Parents, mentors, churches, schools, and student groups.

HOW THIS BOOK WILL HELP STUDENTS

01

Understand that campus freedom requires discipline and personal responsibility.

02

Learn how to manage time, friends, learning habits, and campus decisions.

03

Know the mistakes freshers make and how to avoid them early.

04

Build the mindset for academic excellence, leadership, networks, and growth.
