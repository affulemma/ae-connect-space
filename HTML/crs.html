CHRISTIAN RELIGIOUS STUDIES (CRS)

A Short Story

A student once faced a difficult decision. One choice promised quick success, while the other required honesty and patience. The student remembered lessons learned from religious studies about integrity, responsibility, and character. Years later, that decision became one of the best choices of their life. CRS is not only about religion. It is also about values, character, leadership, and how people make decisions.


What Is CRS?

Christian Religious Studies is the study of the Bible, Christian beliefs, teachings, values, and their impact on individuals and society. It helps students understand moral principles, leadership, relationships, and ethical decision-making.


What Will You Study In SHS?

As an SHS student, you may study topics such as:

  • The Life of Jesus Christ
  • The Teachings of Jesus
  • Old Testament Studies
  • New Testament Studies
  • Leadership and Service
  • Faith and Obedience
  • Christian Ethics
  • Justice and Peace
  • The Church and Society
  • Moral Decision-Making

Career Opportunities

CRS can support careers such as:

  • Teacher
  • Lecturer
  • Counselor
  • Social Worker
  • Human Resource Officer
  • Community Development Officer
  • Public Servant
  • NGO Professional
  • Researcher
  • Religious Leader

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think CRS is only for people who want to become pastors. That is not true. CRS develops leadership, communication, ethical reasoning, conflict resolution, and people-management skills. These qualities are valuable in business, government, education, law, and community leadership.
