SPACE PLANNING

SPACE PLANNING is an important professional skill for INTERIOR DESIGN students because it helps them move beyond classroom theory into practical work, career preparation, and real industry problem-solving.

SPACE PLANNING OVERVIEW

SPACE PLANNING gives students the ability to understand professional tasks, use relevant tools, and produce work that employers, clients, communities, and organizations can actually use. In interior design practice, industry projects, professional services, research, operations, and career development, this skill helps students connect academic knowledge with practical results.

WHY SPACE PLANNING MATTERS TODAY

Today, employers want graduates who can solve problems, use modern tools, communicate clearly, and show evidence of what they can do. SPACE PLANNING matters because it turns knowledge into practical value and helps students compete for internships, projects, and entry-level opportunities.
