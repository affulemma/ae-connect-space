ENVIRONMENTAL MONITORING is an important professional skill for FISHERIES students because it helps them move beyond classroom theory into practical work, career preparation, and real industry problem-solving.

In fisheries practice, industry projects, professional services, research, operations, and career development, this skill helps students connect academic knowledge with practical results.
