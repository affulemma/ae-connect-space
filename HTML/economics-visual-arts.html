ECONOMICS

A Short Story

A talented artist created beautiful products but struggled to make a profit. Another artist understood pricing, customer behavior, costs, and markets. The second artist built a successful business. The difference was Economics.


What Is Economics?

Economics is the study of how individuals, businesses, and governments make decisions about limited resources. It helps people understand money, production, trade, business, and financial decision-making.
