CUSTOMER DEVELOPMENT STAGE

LEARN TO SELL WITHOUT FEAR

Selling is not begging. Selling is helping the right person understand a useful solution.

1
STAGE 1

SALES FUNDAMENTALS

Sales begins with understanding who needs your offer, what problem they have, and how your solution helps them. Learn to greet, ask questions, listen, explain clearly, and follow up.

CAN-DO LESSON: A good seller listens first, then offers value.
NEXT: BUILD IDENTITY
2
STAGE 2

BRANDING

Branding is how people remember and trust your business. It includes your name, look, message, promise, behavior, and customer experience.

Even a small student business can have a clean identity and consistent message.

CAN-DO LESSON: People buy faster when your business looks clear and reliable.
NEXT: UNDERSTAND PEOPLE
3
STAGE 3

CUSTOMER PSYCHOLOGY

Customer psychology helps you understand why people buy. They care about price, trust, convenience, status, fear, urgency, quality, and proof.

Do not only describe your product. Show why it matters to the customer.

CAN-DO LESSON: Sell the benefit, not only the item.
NEXT: WRITE TO PERSUADE
4
STAGE 4

COPYWRITING

Copywriting is writing words that make people understand, trust, and take action. It helps with captions, flyers, WhatsApp messages, product descriptions, and ads.

Use simple words: problem, solution, benefit, proof, price, and clear action.

CAN-DO LESSON: Clear words can turn attention into orders.
NEXT: KEEP CUSTOMERS
5
STAGE 5

CUSTOMER SERVICE

Customer service is how you treat people before, during, and after they buy. Reply well, deliver as promised, apologize when needed, and follow up.

Great service creates repeat customers and referrals.

CAN-DO LESSON: Good service is marketing that customers do for you.
WHAT NEXT?

SELL ONE OFFER CLEARLY

Write one simple sales message and send it to ten people who may need your offer.
