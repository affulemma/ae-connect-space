GENERAL KNOWLEDGE IN ART

A Short Story

A student loved drawing and creating beautiful designs but thought art was only a hobby. Years later, that same passion became a career in graphic design. What started as creativity became a profession. General Knowledge In Art helps students understand the foundations of art, design, creativity, and visual communication.


What Is General Knowledge In Art?

General Knowledge In Art introduces students to the principles, elements, history, and applications of art. It develops creativity, observation, problem-solving, design thinking, and artistic appreciation.


What Will You Study In SHS?

As an SHS student, you may study topics such as:

  • Elements of Art
  • Principles of Design
  • Art Appreciation
  • Art History
  • Drawing Techniques
  • Creative Design
  • Colour Theory
  • Visual Communication
  • African Art
  • Art and Society

Career Opportunities

General Knowledge In Art can lead to careers such as:

  • Graphic Designer
  • Illustrator
  • Creative Director
  • Fashion Designer
  • Interior Designer
  • Product Designer
  • Animator
  • Art Teacher
  • Digital Artist
  • Entrepreneur

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think art is only about drawing. In reality, art is behind advertising, branding, fashion, architecture, product design, animation, filmmaking, digital media, and many creative industries. Creativity has become one of the most valuable skills in today's economy.
