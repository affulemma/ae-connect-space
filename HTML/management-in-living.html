MANAGEMENT IN LIVING

A Short Story

Two students received the same amount of money every month. One always complained about not having enough. The other managed their money, time, and resources wisely and always seemed prepared. The difference was not income. The difference was management. Management In Living teaches practical skills for making the best use of available resources.


What Is Management In Living?

Management In Living focuses on managing personal, family, and community resources effectively. It teaches decision-making, budgeting, planning, consumer education, and responsible living.

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think Management In Living is only about household activities. In reality, it teaches life management skills that help people make better decisions, manage resources effectively, build healthy relationships, and achieve personal goals. These skills are valuable in every profession and stage of life.
