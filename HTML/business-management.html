BUSINESS MANAGEMENT

A Short Story

Two people started similar businesses. Both had money, products, and customers. One business grew rapidly while the other struggled. The difference was not the product. The difference was management. The successful owner knew how to plan, organize, lead people, and make decisions. Business Management teaches these important skills.


What Is Business Management?

Business Management is the study of how organizations are planned, organized, directed, and controlled to achieve their goals. It focuses on leadership, decision-making, communication, strategy, and organizational success.


What Will You Study In SHS?

As an SHS student, you may study topics such as:

  • Management Principles
  • Leadership Styles
  • Business Communication
  • Decision Making
  • Organizational Structure
  • Human Resource Management
  • Marketing Concepts
  • Business Planning
  • Entrepreneurship
  • Business Environment

Career Opportunities

Business Management can lead to careers such as:

  • Business Manager
  • Chief Executive Officer (CEO)
  • Human Resource Manager
  • Project Manager
  • Operations Manager
  • Marketing Manager
  • Business Consultant
  • Entrepreneur
  • Public Administrator
  • Corporate Executive

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think management is only for people who supervise workers. In reality, management is the ability to coordinate people, resources, and opportunities to achieve results. Whether you become a business owner, government official, NGO leader, or corporate executive, management skills will always be valuable.
