PRODUCT SOURCING

PRODUCT SOURCING is an important professional skill for E-COMMERCE students because it helps them move beyond classroom theory into practical work, career preparation, and real industry problem-solving.

PRODUCT SOURCING OVERVIEW

PRODUCT SOURCING gives students the ability to understand professional tasks, use relevant tools, and produce work that employers, clients, communities, and organizations can actually use. In e-commerce practice, industry projects, professional services, research, operations, and career development, this skill helps students connect academic knowledge with practical results.
