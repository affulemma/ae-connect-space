Application Guide

Prepare Early

  • Admission letter or proof of application
  • Academic transcripts and certificates
  • Updated CV or resume
  • Personal statement or study objectives
  • Reference letters from lecturers or supervisors
  • Passport, Ghana Card, or national ID where required
  • Proof of leadership, volunteering, or community work
