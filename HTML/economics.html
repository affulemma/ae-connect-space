ECONOMICS

A Short Story

Imagine two friends receive GH₵100 each. One spends everything immediately. The other saves part, invests part, and spends the rest carefully. Five years later, their situations are completely different. Why? That simple question is one of the many things Economics tries to answer. Economics helps us understand how people, businesses, and governments make decisions when resources are limited.


What Is Economics?

Economics is the study of how individuals, businesses, and governments allocate scarce resources to satisfy unlimited wants. It helps us understand production, consumption, trade, employment, inflation, and economic growth.

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think Economics is only about money. It is not. Economics teaches decision-making. The same principles used to understand countries can also help you understand your personal finances, business decisions, investments, and even career choices. The deeper you study Economics, the more you begin to see how the world really works.
