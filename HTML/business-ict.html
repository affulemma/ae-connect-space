ELECTIVE ICT

A Short Story

A student started a small online business while still in school. At first, sales were slow. After learning spreadsheets, digital marketing, social media management, and online business tools, the student began reaching more customers and managing the business more effectively. Technology became the advantage that helped the business grow.


What Is Elective ICT?

Elective ICT focuses on the use of technology, computers, digital tools, information systems, and communication technologies. It helps students develop digital skills that are increasingly important in modern business and professional environments.


What Will You Study In SHS?

As an SHS student, you may study topics such as:

  • Computer Fundamentals
  • Information Systems
  • Word Processing
  • Spreadsheets
  • Database Concepts
  • Internet Technologies
  • Networking Basics
  • Digital Communication
  • Data Management
  • ICT Project Work

Something Most Students Don't Know

Many students think ICT is only about using computers. In reality, ICT is one of the most valuable skills in today's economy. Businesses, banks, governments, startups, and international organizations all depend on technology. Students who combine business knowledge with digital skills often have a significant advantage in the job market.
