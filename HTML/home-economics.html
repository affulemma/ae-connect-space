HOME ECONOMICS

Home Economics is for students interested in food, nutrition, fashion, textiles, family resource management, hospitality, health, entrepreneurship, and creative industries. The programme combines practical life skills with professional knowledge that can lead to careers in nutrition, fashion, hospitality, healthcare, education, business, and many other fields. Before choosing Home Economics, remember that English Language, Core Mathematics, Integrated Science, and Social Studies are compulsory subjects for all SHS students. Select a subject below to learn more.

🍽️ Food & Nutrition

Learn about food preparation, nutrition, health, hospitality, and diet planning.

👗 Clothing & Textiles

Explore fashion design, garment construction, fabrics, textiles, and clothing production.

🏠 Management In Living

Learn family resource management, budgeting, consumer education, and practical life skills.

🧬 Biology

Understand living organisms, health, nutrition, human systems, and life sciences.

📈 Economics

Learn how individuals, businesses, and governments make economic decisions.

🎨 General Knowledge In Art

Develop creativity, design thinking, artistic appreciation, and visual communication skills.
